One: (English: 1: I am alone) is a 2014 Indian telugu-language psychological thriller film written and directed by sukumar. Produced by ram achanta, gopi chand as 14 Reels Entertainment and distributed by Eros International, the film features maheshbabu and kriti who makes her telugu movie debut, in the lead roles. Mahesh's son, Gautham krishna, made his debut in the film as a younger version .
